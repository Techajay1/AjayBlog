The most common language used to create websites is HTML. HTML is a markup language that is used to generate and maintain web pages.

What are HTML Attributes and How Do I Use Them?

Attributes are data fields that include additional information. Attributes are represented by an opening tag with extra information inside.

An attribute might be something like:

<img src="mydog.jpg" alt="A photo of my dog." width="200" height="200">

The image source (src) ,(alt), and width,height are attributes of the img> tag in this case.
